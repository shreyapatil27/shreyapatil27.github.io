Excel Data Analysis

The dataset used in this analysis is from the Blue Mountain store, a retail establishment offering a diverse range of products catering to all age groups. Operating across multiple e-commerce platforms and serving customers in various states across India, the dataset encapsulates the comprehensive sales data of this dynamic store.

GOAL

Blue Mountain store wants to create an annual sales report for 2022. So that, they can understand their customers and grow more sales in 2023.

INSIGHTS

  • Women are more likely to buy compared to men (~65%)
  • Maharashtra, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh have highest sales
  • Adult age group (30-49 yrs) is max contributor (~50%)
  • Amazon, Flipkart and Myntra channels are major contributors

CONCLUSION

Sales can be improved by tailoring promotions for female customers aged 30-49 residing in Maharashtra, Karnataka and Uttar Pradesh by extending exclusive offers and discounts on Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra.
